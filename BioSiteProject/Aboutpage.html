Bio: How It Started

Tamara was raised in Round Rock, Texas, which is the suburbs of Austin, Texas. She graduated from Thorndale High class of 1993. Her childhood dream career was a teacher because she was fortunate enough to be taught by caring and superlative teachers. In 2001, she graduated from South Plains College as a registered nurse. Each day she aspires to provide the best care for her patients. Her nursing responsibilities include record keeping of patient details and symptoms, administering medications and medicines, assessing smptoms,etc. She held an occupation as a nurse for the Covenant Hospital in Lubbock, Tx for 8 years. Currently, she is a charge nurse at Farwell Care and Rehabilitation Center in Farwell, Texas. May 1, 2022, she received an appreciation award for her dedication and hard work.
Next, Tamara loves to host events as an event planner. She has hosted birthday parties, anniversary celebrations, baby showers, and even a wedding. Decorating and bringing people’s visions alive, of the perfect event is a joy and privilege. Her last event was hosted at the Chateau Cocomar in Houston, Texas on May 21st, 2022, where she wowed the audience with fireworks that sprang in the air as the song “America, The Beautiful” was sung. Another wow factor was the castle cake inspired by the castle in which the event was held in. Her event was rated 5 stars by the guests who attended.

Interview Questions

  1. Who are you? Tamara Ellison. I am a Christian first. I am the eldest child of Kathleen Ellison. The mother of six children and a nurse to all of those who need care.
  2. What are your hobbies?I enjoy going to thrift shops and collecting things from the past. Antiques interest me. I also love making recipes from scratch.
  3. What are your main strengths?I am a good conversationalist, patient and I try to always be kind.
  4. What causes are you passionate about? I am passionate about adoption, especially for young adults that have a more challenging time being adopted. Young adults often face a harder time and need a bit more help because they are beginning to touch the surface of responsibility and daily challenges of what it means to be an adult.
  5. Why do you love your career? I love my career because I enjoy helping others. I strive to make others feel cared or concerned about. Finally, my job allows me to show my strengths such as caring for others and trustworthiness.
  6. If you could have any other career, what would it be? A teacher because every day I see the great need for teachers in so many counties. Yes, working with children can be challenging, but the children being taught today will be the teachers of tomorrow, so I want to ensure they are learning at their best potential.
  7. Who do you admire? I admire people who have succeeded, even when all odds were against them.
  8. What are your goals? My goal is to one day run a women and children shelter. I would also like to own my own restaurant.
  9. What is the hardest challenge in life? Getting everyone to care for one another. Globalization.
  10. What is your dream travel destination? My dream destination is Paris, France because everyone deserves to try the best food. I would love to visit one of the most toured destinations in the world
